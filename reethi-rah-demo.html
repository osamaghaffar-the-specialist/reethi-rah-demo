Getting Here

Your Journey
to Paradise

One&Only Reethi Rah is located in the North Malé Atoll, accessible by speedboat or seaplane from Velana International Airport.

Our dedicated concierge team will arrange all transfers to ensure your journey is as seamless and spectacular as your stay.

    Speedboat Transfer45 minutes from Velana International Airport
